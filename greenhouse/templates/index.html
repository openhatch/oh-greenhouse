Greenhouse is a tool designed to help the Debian Mentor Team in its mission to:


  • Reach out to new contributors, thank them for their work and get feedback.
  • Reach out to people who might be ready to apply for upload rights and help them.
  • Reach out to contributors that went inactive and get feedback from them and offer help.

    • Currently you must be a member of the team to use Greenhouse. In the future, we hope to provide publicly viewable anonymized data that helps give a picture of the Greenhouse contributor community.
